Éco-Smétique Score

National Public System for Environmental, Health and Social Evaluation of Cosmetic Products

Project Market Failure Economic Model Digital Platform Market Study Opinion Survey Roadmap

Project Overview

Éco-Smétique Score is a public national initiative designed to provide a clear and reliable evaluation of the environmental, health and social impacts of cosmetic products.

The system addresses the lack of transparent information available to consumers and aims to encourage the cosmetic industry to improve product formulations.

Main Objectives
  • Help consumers make informed choices
  • Encourage industry transition toward sustainable cosmetics
  • Reduce environmental and health impacts
  • Create a national public information standard

Market Failure

Information Asymmetry

Consumers cannot easily evaluate the real impact of cosmetic ingredients.

Environmental Externalities

Environmental damage is not included in the final product price.

No Common Standard

Multiple private labels create confusion instead of clarity.

Economic Model

The project uses a hybrid financing model combining public funding and industry participation.

Company Type Evaluation Price Justification
Small Companies (SME) 700 € Accessible price to encourage participation
Medium Companies (ETI) 1 400 € Reflects larger production scale
Large Companies 2 800 € Higher capacity and market influence

Re-evaluation Incentive

Products with poor scores can be re-evaluated after improvement.
  • 50% reduced price
  • Encourages reformulation
  • Promotes continuous improvement

National Digital Infrastructure

Public Website

  • Search cosmetic products
  • Compare environmental scores
  • Access scientific explanations
  • Track brand improvements

Mobile Application

  • Instant barcode scanning
  • Score display
  • Better alternatives suggested

Artificial Intelligence System

  • Automatic ingredient analysis
  • Scientific database cross-checking
  • Life-cycle environmental estimation
  • Detection of inconsistencies

All AI models are transparent and scientifically validated.

Market Study

Cosmetic Market Context

  • Global cosmetic market continues to grow rapidly
  • Consumers increasingly demand sustainable products
  • Many brands claim sustainability without standardized proof

Opportunity

A national evaluation system could become a European standard and strengthen the competitiveness of sustainable cosmetic brands.

Opinion Survey Design

The project includes a consumer survey to understand public expectations regarding cosmetic transparency.

Questionnaire (Closed Questions Only)

1. Do you check the ingredients of cosmetic products before buying?



2. Would you like a simple score showing the environmental impact of cosmetics?


3. Would a score influence your purchase decision?


4. Do you trust current cosmetic labels?


5. Would you scan a product with your phone to know its score?
